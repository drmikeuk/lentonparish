Room bookings

We have two flexible spaces that can be hired at a competitive rate for a variety of activities.

Priory Church

The Priory is a flexible space with seating for about 60 people and a small coffee bar.
Email bookings@lentonparish.org.uk

Unity House

Unity House has two meeting rooms, a large well equipped kitchen and a spacious garden. The larger meeting room accommodates about 50 people, and has French windows opening onto the garden and the smaller meeting room can seat up to 15 people.
Email bookings@lentonparish.org.uk
